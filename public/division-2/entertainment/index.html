What the Entertainment Hub Covers

The Entertainment hub collects Division 2 story, lore, edits, short films, commentary, community projects, fan content, and creator-driven broadcasts that are not strictly builds, tools, or guides.

This section helps keep entertainment content discoverable without mixing it into build and guide searches where agents are looking for direct gameplay help.

Entertainment Content Types

  • Division 2 lore, story, and atmosphere content.
  • Community edits, fan projects, and cinematic videos.
  • Creator commentary and entertainment-focused broadcasts.
  • Shorts and videos that are better classified by format or intent rather than build category.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why separate entertainment from guides and builds?

Entertainment content serves a different purpose than build planning or gameplay guides, so separating it keeps the library easier to browse.

Can a creator appear in entertainment and build sections?

Yes. A creator can publish multiple content types, and each video should be organized by its actual intent.
