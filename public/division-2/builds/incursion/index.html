What Makes an Incursion Build Work

Incursion builds are designed around coordinated group content where each agent needs a clear role. Damage matters, but team survival, boss mechanics, add control, healing, and positioning can be just as important.

The best Incursion-ready builds usually support a specific job in the encounter. Some agents focus on sustained DPS, while others bring healing, tanking, crowd control, debuffs, or encounter-specific utility.

Best Use Cases for Incursion Builds

  • Paradise Lost group runs and boss encounters.
  • DPS roles where the group needs reliable damage windows.
  • Tank or survivability roles where pressure needs to be absorbed.
  • Healer and support roles that keep the group alive during mechanics.
  • Agents learning Incursion mechanics before moving into faster clears.
  • Teams testing raid-style coordination and role assignments.

Common Incursion Build Roles

  • Weapon DPS builds for boss damage and add clear.
  • Tank builds that survive high pressure and protect team positioning.
  • Healer builds that keep teammates alive through damage spikes.
  • Support builds that boost team damage or weaken enemies.
  • Crowd-control builds that help control dangerous waves or priority targets.

Recommended Incursion Setup Notes

Incursion builds should be tested with the team role in mind. A build that performs well in normal PvE may still need changes for Paradise Lost because mechanics, boss phases, and team responsibilities can demand different gear choices.

When comparing Incursion builds on DivGaming, look for role clarity, group synergy, survivability tools, damage windows, and whether the setup was designed for learning runs or optimized clears.

Common Incursion Build Mistakes

  • Bringing a generic PvE build without understanding the encounter role.
  • Ignoring team composition and duplicating roles the group already has.
  • Building only for damage when the team needs survivability or support.
  • Using a solo setup without adjusting for group mechanics.
  • Skipping utility that would make boss phases or add waves safer.

Frequently Asked Questions

What roles are useful in Division 2 Incursion builds?

Most Incursion groups benefit from strong DPS plus role support such as tank, healer, crowd control, or encounter-specific utility.

Can Incursion builds be used outside Paradise Lost?

Some Incursion builds work in other PvE activities, but the best results usually come from adjusting the build to the activity.

Do Incursion builds need a full team?

Incursion content is designed around coordinated group play, so builds should usually be selected with team roles and encounter mechanics in mind.

What should new Incursion players bring?

New Incursion players usually benefit from reliable DPS or support builds with enough survivability to learn mechanics without constantly going down.
