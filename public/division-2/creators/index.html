What the Creator Hub Covers

The Creator hub highlights Division 2 creators, build specialists, guide makers, broadcasters, and SHD network contributors featured on DivGaming. It gives agents a cleaner way to discover who is producing the videos, builds, guides, and resources surfaced across the site.

Creator pages help connect individual channels and communities to the builds, guides, tips, and broadcasts agents find in the DivGaming library.

How Agents Can Use Creator Pages

  • Find creators who specialize in PvE builds, PvP builds, solo play, guides, news, tips, or tools.
  • Move from a creator profile back into build and video hubs.
  • Discover new channels and resource contributors from the broader Division 2 community.
  • Support community discovery without burying creators inside one large video list.
