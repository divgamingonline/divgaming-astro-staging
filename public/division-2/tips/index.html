What the Division 2 Tips Hub Covers

The Tips hub is for fast, practical Division 2 advice. It supports agents who want shorter explanations, farming reminders, combat help, build notes, vendor checks, Dark Zone guidance, or new-agent recommendations.

Tips are especially useful when agents need a quick answer before changing a build, starting a mission, farming an exotic, checking vendors, or testing a role.
